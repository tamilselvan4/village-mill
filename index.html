The Difference

Why Wood Pressed?

Modern refining strips oils of everything that makes them alive. We don't do that.

🌿

Wood Pressed (Our Way)

  • ✦ Pressed below 40°C — nutrients intact
  • ✦ Natural colour, aroma, flavour preserved
  • ✦ Rich in Vitamin E & antioxidants
  • ✦ Unfiltered, unbleached, undeodorized
  • ✦ Traditional wooden chekku method
  • ✦ Single-origin traceable seeds
VS
⚗️

Refined (Their Way)

  • ✗ Heated above 200°C — nutrients destroyed
  • ✗ Bleached, deodorized, stripped
  • ✗ Chemical solvents (hexane) used
  • ✗ Trans fats may form during processing
  • ✗ No flavour, no aroma, no character
  • ✗ Additives and preservatives added

The Craft

Traditional Extraction
Process

Six ancient steps. Zero shortcuts.

01

Seed Selection

Only the finest, single-origin, naturally grown seeds are chosen — selected by hand for size, colour, and freshness.

02

Sun Drying

Seeds are spread on clean jute mats and dried under natural sunlight for 2–3 days to achieve the perfect moisture balance.

03

Chekku Loading

The dried seeds are fed into the ancient wooden chekku, a heavy-duty rotating press carved from a single piece of hardwood.

04

Slow Rotation

The wooden pestle rotates gently at 5–8 RPM, generating no excess heat. The entire process takes 2–4 hours per batch.

05

Natural Extraction

Golden oil flows naturally through the carved channel into a clay pot below — no pressure, no heat, no chemicals. Just gravity and patience.

06

Bottled with Care

The oil rests for 24 hours for natural sediment to settle, then gently poured into glass bottles — sealed, labelled, and ready to nourish.
